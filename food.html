My Favourite Food

1. Chicken Rice

My first favourite food is chicken rice. Chicken rice contains a delighful dishes such as the yellow rice with a roasted chicken accompanying with a slice of cucumber and tomato. Also, with a cup of soup. The taste levels depend on the restaurant that made it because some restaurant didn't make it well.

2. Nasi Kandar

My next favourite food is nasi kandar. The name nasi kandar came about from a time when nasi (rice) hawkers or vendors would balance a "kandar pole" on their shoulder with two huge containers of rice meals. The name has remained today and Nasi Kandar's word appears in most Tamil Muslim or “Malaysian Mamak” restaurants and Indian-Muslim food stall. In nasi kandar you can put whatever dishes you like for example fried chicken, papadom, kambing masak kurma and many more. For me my dishes is a plate of nasi kandar with extra rice with fried chicken and papadom.

3. Nasi Lemak Kuning

This is my favourite breakfast. People around me would rather call it a brunch because the dishes portion is like eating lunch. Nasi lemak kuning has a fragrance aroma that no other nasi lemak has on it. In my portion, I would like to eat it with fried chicken or "daging masak hitam". The store locate near old cinema called Kapitol. Its open from 7 a.m till 1 p.m and it's the oldest restaurant in Parit Buntar.

4. Vanilla Icecream

Lastly, my favourite light desert that is vanilla icecream. Everytime I buy an icecream I would prefer vanilla first. I'm not only eating vanilla icecream only but also eat with chocolate syrup or chocolate chip.
